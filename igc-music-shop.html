Our Story

About IGC Music

Welcome to IGC The Music Shop, your go-to destination for musical instruments and audio equipment in Kalwa, Thane. Located at Sudama Nagar, Manisha Nagar, we've been serving musicians, students and music lovers with passion and expertise.

From beginners picking up their first guitar to seasoned professionals looking for studio-grade microphones and karaoke systems — we have something for everyone. Our team believes that music is for everyone, and we are here to help you find your perfect sound.

We stock hundreds of instruments from trusted brands, offer in-store repair services, and ensure a warm, welcoming experience for every customer who walks through our doors.
